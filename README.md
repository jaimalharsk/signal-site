# The Signal

**The Signal** is a decentralized publishing interface built around three channels of transmission.

It combines **local-first writing**, **content-addressed storage**, and a **minimal broadcast interface** for creator-owned distribution.

---

## Channels

The Signal organizes content into three broadcast channels:

### Wavelength
Long-form essays and deep technical thinking.

### Flashburst
Temporary drops such as releases, experiments, and announcements.

### Driftline
Narrative logs, lessons learned, and creator stories.

Each post becomes a **transmission** stored on IPFS and referenced by its CID.
